Regulatory Advice

After the banking crisis of 2008 the regulatory requirements for banks grew significantly in terms of complexity and implementation costs.

We can help you to fulfill these regulatory requirements in an cost efficient way by our extensive experience with Basel III and FRTB programs in various major financial institutions.

Case Study 1: Back-Testing Policy for IMM Credit Risk Models

The Client's Requirements

The client, a global South African bank, planned to apply for IMM under Basel III. The IMM waiver required the institution to introduce a back-testing framework including principles, policies and procedures.

Our Solution

Based on our expericnce with an IMM application to the UK FSA, we helped the client to define a robust back-testing framework, which was practical and fulfilled the regulatory requirements.

The Client's Benefits

In addition to being able to meet the regulatory requirements, we provided to the firm a framework, which allows to quantify the quality of the model in economic terms. In addition to providing a pass/fail statement for the model performance, we were able to give to management a cash denominated estimate of the serverity of the model's shortcomings.
