# Models The internal models used to calculate regulatory capital need to be fit for purpose. We can gain confidence, that the models, are appropriate by having - a structured model development process - an effective model approval process - and internal audit reviews in place. We can help you in each of these area to find solutions, which are suitable for you particular institution. # Implementation It must be made sure, that the models are appropriately implemented in the firm's risk system. This involves - Specification - Writing - Testing - Roll-out We can assist you to implement these steps in an efficient and appropriate way. # Policies and Governance We can help you making sure, that the firm's governance and policies are compliant with Basel III and FRTB requirements. # IMM Application An IMM application aims to present a high level narrative of the principles of the firm's risk management framework, backed with evidence, that this framework is fit for purpose. We can support you in the application process by sharing out first hand involvement in discussions with regulators and in composing such applications.
